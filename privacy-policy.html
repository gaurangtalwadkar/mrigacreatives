Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 15, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to MriGa Creatives. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy applies to our mobile applications, including "Bingo Number Generator" and "MriGa Bingo Number Generator".

2. Data Collection and Ads

Bingo Number Generator integrates Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect and use data to serve personalized ads.

For our other apps that do not show ads, we do not strictly collect personal data. However, for apps using AdMob, certain device identifiers and usage data may be collected by Google.

3. Permissions

Our apps request internet access to load advertisements via AdMob. We also request permissions necessary for core functionality (e.g., keeping the screen awake during gameplay).

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party service to serve advertisements:

  • Google AdMob: Google may use the advertising ID from the device to generate interests and demographics (for example, 'sports enthusiasts'). Interests, demographics, and other data may be used to serve better targeted ads to you.

You can view Google's Privacy Policy here: https://policies.google.com/privacy

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Thus, you are advised to review this page periodically for any changes. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us.
