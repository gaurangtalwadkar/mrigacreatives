Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 15, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to MriGa Creatives. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy applies to our mobile applications, including "Bingo Number Generator" and "MriGa Bingo Number Generator".

2. Data Collection

Our applications are designed to be simple, offline utilities. We do not collect, store, or share any personal data from our users.

We do not require you to create an account, log in, or provide any personal information to use our apps.

3. Permissions

Our apps may request minimal permissions necessary for their core functionality (e.g., preventing the screen from sleeping while a game is in progress). These permissions are not used to access any private data on your device.

4. Third-Party Services

Since our apps function offline and do not serve advertisements, we do not integrate with third-party tracking or advertising services that collect user data.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Thus, you are advised to review this page periodically for any changes. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us.
